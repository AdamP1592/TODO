# Growth Cone Dynamics
A developmental model of growth cone dynamics to be utilized in a unified develepmental sim I've been researching for development for a considerable amount of time.

## Reasoning
This is a passion project. I have a strong interest in biology and simulation. A developmental model of a neuron culture has been a long time goal towards something even greater, but you can only walk one step at a time. This project was selected to translate the knowledge I've gained into an internal model that I can intuitively use. This will be designed to allow integration with Cable-theory or Compartmental neuron models. 

Relates to my steady-state neuron culture simulation. [here](https://github.com/AdamP1592/Network-Simulation).
